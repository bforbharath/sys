How to Protect from flood

Put together a disaster supply kit that includes essential items such as water, non-perishable food, a first aid kit, flashlights, batteries, a portable radio, extra clothing, blankets, and any necessary medications. Include important documents, identification, and contact information in waterproof and easily accessible containers.

How to Protect from Drought

Practice water conservation by reducing your water usage at home. Fix any leaks promptly, install water-efficient fixtures and appliances, and be mindful of your water consumption habits. Use water-saving techniques such as collecting rainwater for irrigation or reusing greywater for non-potable purposes like watering plants.!
